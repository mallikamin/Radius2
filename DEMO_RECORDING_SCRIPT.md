## Recording Tips

1. **Keep each scene under 90 seconds** — attention spans are short
2. **Use the demo data** — it has realistic Pakistani names, PKR amounts, Faisalabad projects
3. **Click slowly** — viewers need to see what you're clicking
4. **Narrate the "why" not the "how"** — "This solves X problem" beats "Click here then here"
5. **Total target:** 12-15 minutes for the full walkthrough
6. **Music:** Subtle background track, lower during narration
